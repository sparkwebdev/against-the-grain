Our Story

Jono’s passion for brewing and cider making goes back over a decade, with three years’ experience following his passion as a professional brewer. However, his West Country roots, and the lack of interesting cider in London, drove home to pack-in the grain and strike out on his own.

Well, not on his own.

Conor comes from an entrepreneurial background working within the catering industry and, over quite a few pints with Jono, got pretty excited about changing the face of London’s cider.

After the hangover, they teamed up to create Against The Grain, a micro-cider based in South West London. Crafting exciting and innovative ciders, they are intending to propel the world of cider to new heights.
